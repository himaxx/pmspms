## 📋 A — New Requirement Form

### ❌ Before
```
Worker fills Google Form
    ↓
Form submitted → silence
    ↓
Worker has NO idea:
  • Was a job number created?
  • Who is handling it?
  • What's the current status?

To find out → must open Google Sheets
            → scroll through 100s of rows
            → workers are NOT technical!
```

### ✅ After
```

    ↓

    ↓

  ┌─────────────────────────────┐
  │  Job #247 — Capri Full Fit  │
  │  🟡 Step 2 — Awaiting       │
  │     Production Approval     │
  └─────────────────────────────┘
```

| Pain Point | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| Job Number | Manual, error-prone | ✅ Auto-generated |
| Status visible? | ❌ No | ✅ Yes, instantly |
| Worker needs Sheets? | ❌ Yes (hard) | ✅ No |

## 🚚 D — Naame (Giving to Thekedar)

### ❌ Before
```
Job is dispatched to thekedar
    ↓
Recorded in a register (physical book)
    ↓
No lead time tracking
    ↓
No deadline set
    ↓
After a week...
    ↓
Manager calls thekedar: "Maal kab aayega?"
    ↓
Thekedar: "Kal tak"  (same answer every day)
    ↓
No real visibility of what's pending with whom
```

### ✅ After
```
Dispatch person opens PMS Pro → "Step 4 — Naame"
    ↓
Selects thekedar from registered list
    ↓
Enters: pieces sent, lead time (e.g. 5 days)
    ↓
System automatically calculates:
  Expected Jama Date = Dispatch Date + Lead Time
    ↓
After lead time expires → job turns 🔴 RED (Late)
    ↓
Manager sees at a glance:
  ┌────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  🔴 LATE  Job #247 — Capri        │
  │  With: Mukati Ji                  │
  │  Expected Jama: 2 May (overdue)   │
  └────────────────────────────────────┘
```

| Pain Point | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| Who has which job? | ❌ Check register | ✅ See on app instantly |
| Deadlines tracked? | ❌ No | ✅ Auto-calculated & monitored |
| Late alerts? | ❌ Only after chaos | ✅ App flags it proactively 🚨 |
| Thekedar workload? | ❌ No idea | ✅ "Thekedar Insights" view |

## 📦 E — Jama (Receiving Finished Goods)

### ❌ Before
```
Goods come back from thekedar
    ↓
Count pieces manually
    ↓
Check the register / Google Sheet
    ↓
Register is hard to read
    ↓
Calculate remaining balance manually
    ↓
Write it down somewhere
    ↓
Errors in piece count → disputes with thekedar
    ↓
Difficult to reconcile at end of month
```

### ✅ After
```
Goods arrive → open PMS Pro → "Step 5 — Jama"
    ↓
Select job (only jobs with thekedar are shown)
    ↓
Enter quantity received
    ↓
System auto-calculates:
  Balance = Pieces Sent − Pieces Received
    ↓
If balance > 0 → job stays active
  If balance = 0 → job moves to Settle
    ↓
Everything is recorded: date, qty, balance
    ↓
No more disputes — the numbers are always there
```

| Pain Point | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| Piece count accuracy | ❌ Manual, error-prone | ✅ System calculates balance |
| Reading the register | ❌ Hard & fragile | ✅ Clean, searchable digital record |
| Reconciliation | ❌ Monthly headache | ✅ Always up to date |
